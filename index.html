Presentation of our programs

Three different programmes are proposed :

1. Fruity Program : This program runs for 4 hours. It allows you to learn the basics of music creation with the FL Studio software.

2. Producer Program : This program takes place over a weekend (10 hours). It allows you to deepen your knowledge on various software such as FL Studio or Ableton.

3. Advanced Program : This program runs for one week (7 hours per day). It allows you to improve your knowledge on various software such as FL Studio or Ableton, and also to help you launch your career as a producer or DJ.
